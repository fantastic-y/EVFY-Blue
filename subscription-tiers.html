Roadmap

Our Sustainability Index is a program that provides green conscious members the platform to make informed carbon reduction decisions, with levels of services that consumers are comfortable with.

As part of the program, EVFY offers consumers subscription tiers and a roadmap to make the most informed decisions. This includes monthly reports, access to green products and services and offers a forum for members to share green lifestyle tips and a community to help others to become more ‘green.’

EVFY Provides Sustainable Lifestyle Choices.

The Sustainability Index assigns a green score to everyday products and services so you can have a consolidated sustainability score. Now you can have an easy way to see how your day-to-day decisions impact the world around you. You can also have fun sharing your score with friends and family too!

Choose Your Sustainability Tier.

Simply select the sustainability tier that works best for you and your ‘green’ lifestyle. The more sustainable you are, the higher your sustainability index will become. This ensures that you will also receive the highest discounts and rewards from our favorite sustainable partners.
